Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship across India?

Yes, we ship to all major cities and towns across India via reliable courier partners. Delivery usually takes 3-5 working days.

Is the oil packed in plastic?

Yes, it is packed in high-grade, food-safe plastic. We strictly use premium quality, virgin PET bottles that are certified BPA-free.

Can I visit the store?

Absolutely! We are located in Pattanam Pudur, Coimbatore. You can visit us to see our products and process.

How do I track my order?

Once shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and Email to monitor your package in real-time.
